Our Story

Built by Passion, Driven by Quality

T&W Auto Detailing was built by two hardworking guys with a shared passion for cars and a commitment to doing things the right way.

What started as a simple idea—providing high-quality detailing with real attention to detail—has grown into a business focused on delivering results our customers can trust.

Based in Central Coast & Newcastle, we specialize in professional interior and exterior detailing for cars, trucks, SUVs, boats, bikes, and more. As a two-man operation, we take pride in being hands-on with every vehicle. No shortcuts, no rushed jobs—just honest work and clean results.
