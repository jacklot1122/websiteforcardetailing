What We Do

Premium Detailing Services

From ceramic coatings to full interior details, we've got you covered

Ceramic Coating

Our premium ceramic coating service provides long-lasting protection for your vehicle's paintwork. This advanced nanotechnology creates a hydrophobic layer that repels water, dirt, and contaminants while enhancing your car's shine.

  • Long-lasting protection (2+ years)
  • Hydrophobic water-repelling finish
  • Enhanced gloss and depth
  • UV protection
  • Easier cleaning and maintenance

Paint Correction

Our paint correction service removes swirl marks, light scratches, water spots, and other defects to restore your vehicle's paint to its original glory. Using professional-grade compounds and polishers, we carefully bring back the depth and clarity of your paintwork.

  • Removes swirl marks & scratches
  • Eliminates water spots & oxidation
  • Restores paint clarity and depth
  • Single or multi-stage correction
  • Professional compounds & techniques
View Pricing

Interior Detailing

Transform your vehicle's interior with our comprehensive deep cleaning service. We meticulously clean every surface, from carpets and upholstery to dashboards and door panels, leaving your interior fresh, sanitized, and looking like new.

  • Deep vacuum and steam cleaning
  • Leather cleaning and conditioning
  • Dashboard & console detailing
  • Window & mirror cleaning
  • Odor elimination
View Pricing

Exterior Wash & Wax

Our hand wash and wax service provides a thorough clean and protective layer for your vehicle's exterior. Using pH-neutral shampoos and premium wax, we ensure a showroom-quality finish every time.

  • Hand wash with pH-neutral shampoo
  • Wheel and tire cleaning
  • Clay bar treatment (optional)
  • Premium wax application
  • Window and trim dressing
View Pricing

Headlight Restoration

Cloudy, yellowed headlights not only look bad but also reduce visibility and safety. Our headlight restoration service removes oxidation and haze, restoring clarity and brightness to your headlights.

  • Removes oxidation and yellowing
  • Restores clarity and brightness
  • Improves nighttime visibility
  • UV sealant protection
  • Long-lasting results
